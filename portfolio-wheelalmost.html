I develop accessible pathways for exploring science and technology through artistic, historical, and cultural artifacts. As a multidisciplinary scholar-artist, architectural designer, and instructor of multimediatic literature, my critical-creative practice explores how marginalized positionalities drive digital innovation and how these technologies shape society.
